Michael Jones-Correa

President's Distinguished Professor of Political Science
Department of Political Science, University of Pennsylvania

I study political participation and civic engagement; urban politics; inter-ethnic contact, trust and civic engagement; immigrant political incorporation, naturalization and political mobilization; Latino politics and public opinion; and racial and ethnic politics and identity.
